Frequently Asked Questions

My streak reset — can I restore it?

Streaks are tracked locally on your device. If you missed a day or reinstalled the app, unfortunately the streak cannot be recovered. We know it's disappointing — keep going, you've got this!

The timer stopped when my screen locked — what happened?

Bloomodoro uses a Live Activity to display the timer on your lock screen and Dynamic Island during active sessions. If you don't see it, make sure you have Live Activities enabled in Settings → Bloomodoro.

Can I use Bloomodoro on iPad or Mac?

Bloomodoro is currently iPhone-only. iPad and Mac support may come in a future update.
