Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

Overview

Bloomodoro is designed to work entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data we do not collect

  • No account registration or login
  • No personal information (name, email, location)
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No third-party SDKs that collect data

Data stored on your device

All app data — focus sessions, streaks, settings, and plant progress — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Notifications

Bloomodoro may send local notifications to alert you when a session ends. These notifications are processed entirely on your device. No notification data is transmitted to external servers.

Children's Privacy

Bloomodoro does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the date above and post the new version here.
